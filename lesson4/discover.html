Draper City Park

Draper City Park is an eventful recreation area with wide open fields with a great view of the Wasatch Mountains. The annual Draper Arts & Crafts Festival is hosted here during the summer where many food trucks and small businesses set up shop. During the winter, the landscape is transformed to a spectacular display of christmas lights, which is a highlight for many visitors.

Draper City Park is located at 12500 S 1300 E, Draper, Utah 84020.

Loveland Living Planet Aquarium

This marvelous building was opened to the public on March 24, 2014, bringing the The Loveland Living Planet Aquarium to Draper, Utah. The aquarium houses native Utah species and creatures from around the world. This family-friendly facility attracts over 850,000 visitors each year.

Loveland Living Planet Aquarium is located at 12033 S Lone Peak Pkwy, Draper, Utah 84020

Draper Temple

The iconic architecture of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints' Draper, Utah Temple can be seen across the Salt Lake Valley, residing on the side of the Wassatch Mountains. This temple began construction after the temple's groundbreaking on August 5, 2006 and was later dedicated on March 20, 2009. The Draper Temple is one of five latter day saint temples in the Salt Lake Valley, which includes the Salt Lake Temple, Taylorsville Temple, Jordan River Temple, and the Oquirh Mountain Temple. When the sun goes down, the temple's exterior lights illuminates the structure, making it easy to spot at night.

The Draper Temple is located at 14065 S Canyon Vista Ln, Draper, UT 84020

Bear Canyon Suspension Bridge

Along the Orson Smith trailhead lies the Bear Canyon Bridge. The trail goes alongside the foothills of the Wasatch Mountains and connects to the East Bench and Hidden Valley trailheads. There are plenty of elevated roads to travel on which provide an overlook view of the Salt Lake Valley. The bridge finished construction in 2015 and has become a staple landmark of Draper ever since.

The Bear Canyon Bridge is located at 12625 E Highland Dr, Draper, UT 84020

Draper Peaks Shopping Center

The Draper Peaks Shopping Center is home to many retail businesses and restaurants. Venture a little further and you will find Cinemark Draper and XD, Cowabunga Bay, Allstar Bowling, and many more attractions.

The Draper Peaks Shopping Center is located at 200 E 12300th Draper, Utah 84020
