Site Purpose

This site's purpose is to connect with small businesses and to help create networking opportunities for participating companies and the local community. Visitors to the site who are not yet part of this chamber of commerce should feel openly invited to join the community and locate their business to the city of Draper, Utah. The Draper Commerce site will provide visitors with a brief glance of the city, local events, and will help bring attention to local businesses.
